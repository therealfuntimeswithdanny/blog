Raft

By Daniel Morrisey, May 28, 2025

Save to raindrop.io


I’ve played a lot of games. My steam library has over 20 games, but the first game I evered played on Steam was Raft. I found the game by watching Mister Joker on Twitch play with Skizzle Man. The game looked fun. The nice calm relaxing peaceful music holds a place in my heart and will forever.

For the next week I watched Joker and Skizz play raft. The game was calm and peaceful most of the time. They were having fun together. The game looked amazing even at low settings because I was playing on some Intel i3 with 8 gigs of ram.

But the best part of the game was the story line, about 1 year and 6 months ago me and my friend Delano finished the game, we beat Raft the Final chapter! Evan playing alone was fun getting upgrades and just building evan to this day I still play raft by myself but I really can’t wait to play again with people but this time not beat the game on peaceful mode but beat it the right way on normal mode. Since 2022 I have loved the game but adding a second chapter or making a Raft part 2 will be even better!
