Bluesky Adds a new Form of Verification

By Daniel Morrisey, Last Updated: May 27, 2025

Share

Save to raindrop.io

Bluesky adds verification blue check marks to verify accounts! Now if your account has been verified you will see a blue check mark next to your name (display name).

A verified account will have the simple blue check mark a “Trusted Verifiers” account will have an Scalloped Check mark (also blue). A trusted verifier can select accounts to be verified, example: The New York Times can now issue blue checks to its journalists.

The fastest way to get a simple blue check mark is to verify your domain via Bluesky DNS records. An example of an verified domain account is @madebydanny.uk since we own the domain and used DNS records to verify, an non Domain Account is example.bsky.social, as of now April 21 2025 you can’t request an Blue Check mark as Bluesky said they will after they get feedback!
