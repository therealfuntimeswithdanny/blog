Is RSS a new way to search?

By Daniel Morrisey, Jun 3, 2025

Share

Save to raindrop.io


With searching the web becoming a hot mess of AI overview that are wrong and hundreds of ads that don’t help is RSS a new way to search.

Believe it or not people do, most news outlets have a RSS feed and most developers that own blogs or podcast probably do have a RSS Feed, me for example I have one (madebydanny.uk/RSS). But you can’t search for RSS feeds and most RSS feeds are XML so it’s just a bunch of Text to a non developer. But that can change services like Feedle (feedle.world) turn that Plain text into a nice easy to use interface with a search bar and everything.

This lets you find new RSS feeds for your favourite blogs, podcasts and news outlets. Without stupid AI overviews that are wrong and ads that don’t help. All with a easy to use intrface!
