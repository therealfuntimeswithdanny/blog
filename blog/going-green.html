Going Green!

By Daniel Morrisey, Published: May 30, 2025

Save to raindrop.io


Funtimes Media has made the switch to only use Cloudflare Data centers using 100% renewable energy. We did this because our mission is to make apps & websites that are fast, free and don’t hurt the Planet. This means that every request to the domain madebydanny.uk is sent using Cloudflare’s “Green Compute” a subset of Cloudflare Servers and Data Centers using renewable energy. What Does this mean? This means that requests & page views may take longer to load and may have a higher latency, this is because there is less data centers to use making latency higher because Cloudflare will connect to the best Green Compute data center which may be miles away. !
