How to Bluesky

By Daniel Morrisey, May 28, 2025

I’ve used bluesky for about 3 months now. That's not long but I think it’s long enough to write about it. I first made my Bluesky account to follow my local weather station Capital Weather Gang, then I started posting like every 2 days once I found out about Buffer then everything kicked off I got my first follower over the past 3 months my follower count has slowly gone up from 0 to 80 a started to enge with users mainly Buffer and It’s Not Jay. So I made this guide on how to Bluesky.

Getting Started The first thing to do is set up your profile Add a user Name and Profile Photo with a Small Bio adding a banner image makes your profile look more professional but it’s not needed. In my opinion the best way to make yourself stand out is by adding a custom domain to your Bluesky account not by using some free online service like MBDIO to link one but link a domain for your portfolio, blog, photo library etc.

Posting To make posts stand out adding content such as A link with a Banner Image, Photo and/or video to grab the user's attention make your first post be a bio about yourself and pin it to your profile so users can quickly see it.

Getting speed Your post should not be Low effort or spam posting, think about your post and add tags related to your post to appear in feeds and to get recommended to users. Keep on doing that till you start to gain followers. One of the best ways to gain followers is by replying to posts from accounts with more followers but make sure your replies are related and will gain likes.

If you keep on doing that your follower count will increase.
